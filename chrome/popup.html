Human Headers

Embed yourself in the very fabric of the web.

Human Headers is a project to let developers put a little more of themselves into their work without visibly impacting the web.

To implement your own Human Headers on your project, simply append headers to your responses that match:

X-Human-*

When you visit a website using Human Headers, you will receive a popup with the message from the developer who left it.
